Just open Superset Sheets, add a percentage in the weight column, and we’ll auto-calculate the weights for your clients.
Best of all? You don't have to ask your clients for their one rep max.

When you program with percents, we’ll ask your clients to add their 1RM so we can do the calculations for you.
